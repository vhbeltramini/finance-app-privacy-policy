# Privacy Policy - Gestor Financeiro

Effective date: 2026-05-03

This Privacy Policy describes how Gestor Financeiro ("Gestor Financeiro", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects personal information when you use our mobile application and related services.

By creating an account or using the app, you acknowledge this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Who We Are

Gestor Financeiro is a personal finance management app that allows users to register an account and organize financial data such as transactions, categories, payment methods, budgets, and savings goals.

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

- Account and profile information, such as name and email address.
- Authentication information, such as password hash and session tokens.
- Financial content you provide, including transactions, categories, payment methods, budgets, goals, and analytics derived from this data.
- Technical and usage data required to operate the service, such as request metadata, basic device/app diagnostics, and security audit events.
- Settings/preferences stored on your device, such as theme, language, and local session state.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data beyond what is required for core app functionality.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use information to:

- create and manage your account;
- authenticate you and secure your session;
- provide core financial management features;
- generate summaries and analytics requested by you;
- maintain app reliability, prevent abuse, and investigate security events;
- respond to user support requests;
- comply with legal obligations.

## 4. Legal Bases (where applicable)

Depending on your jurisdiction, we process data under one or more of the following legal bases:

- contract performance (to provide the service you requested);
- legitimate interests (security, fraud prevention, service improvement);
- legal obligations (compliance with applicable laws);
- consent (where required by law for specific processing).

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data.

We do not share personal data with third parties for behavioral advertising.

We may share data only when necessary with:

- infrastructure and cloud service providers acting on our behalf;
- legal/regulatory authorities where required by law;
- professional advisors when needed for legal/security matters.

All third-party access is limited to what is necessary to operate or protect the service.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain personal data while your account is active and for as long as needed to provide services and fulfill legal, security, and accounting obligations.

When account deletion is requested and completed, personal account data is deleted or anonymized, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate security purposes.

## 7. Account and Data Deletion

You can request deletion directly in the app using the account deletion flow in Security settings.

You may also contact support at our [Email](finance.app007@gmail.com).

For security, we may request proof of account ownership before completing deletion requests.

After deletion, access to the account is removed. As part of our deletion workflow, we remove audit records linked to the account and keep only the minimal deletion audit event needed to prove that the deletion request was executed (unless broader retention is required by law).

## 8. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, including access controls, authenticated API access, and audit logging for critical account actions.

No system is completely secure, but we continuously improve safeguards and incident response procedures.

## 9. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries different from your country of residence. When applicable, we use appropriate safeguards to protect transferred personal data.

## 10. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13 years old (or a higher minimum age required by local law). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us so we can take appropriate action.
